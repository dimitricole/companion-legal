Privacy Policy

Program: Companion Check-In Messaging · Operated by Dimitri Restaino · Last updated June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Companion Check-In Messaging (“we,” “us”), a personal, invitation-only supportive text-messaging service operated by Dimitri Restaino, collects and uses information from individuals who have opted in to receive our messages.

Information we collect

We collect only what is needed to provide the service:

  • Your mobile phone number.
  • The content of the text messages you exchange with the service.

We do not collect location data, financial information, or data from any source other than your own messages.

How we use your information

We use your phone number and messages solely to operate the service you opted into: to send supportive check-in messages, respond to your messages, and maintain the context of your conversation over time. We do not use your information for advertising or marketing of any kind.

How we share your information

We do not sell or rent your information. Mobile opt-in information, phone numbers, and consent are not shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We use a small number of trusted service providers strictly to operate the service — a messaging carrier to deliver SMS and an AI provider to help generate replies — and they process your information only to provide that functionality, never for their own marketing.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your messages only as long as needed to provide the service. You may request that we delete your information at any time by contacting us at the address below.

Opting out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us at the email below.
