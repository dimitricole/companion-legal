Program description

Companion Check-In Messaging is a personal, invitation-only supportive text-messaging service. It provides conversational wellness check-ins and emotional-support messages, by SMS, to individuals who have personally opted in to receive them. This is not a marketing or promotional program.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies and depends on your conversation. This is a recurring, two-way messaging program.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply. Charges are determined by your mobile carrier and plan.

How to get help

For help at any time, reply HELP to any message, or contact us at dimitri.code@gmail.com.

How to opt out

You can cancel at any time by replying STOP. After you reply STOP, you will receive one confirmation message and then no further messages unless you opt in again.

Carrier liability

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Not a medical or emergency service

This service offers supportive companionship only. It is not a substitute for professional medical, mental health, or emergency care, and it does not provide diagnosis or treatment. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing an emergency, contact your local emergency services (in the U.S., call 911).
